# 📘 Digital Literacy Awareness

## 📌 Overview
Digital Literacy Awareness focuses on educating individuals about the effective and safe use of digital technologies. It helps people understand how to use the internet, digital devices, and online platforms responsibly.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To promote safe and responsible internet usage  
- To develop basic digital skills among students  
- To create awareness about online security and privacy  

---

## 📚 Topics Covered
- What is Digital Literacy  
- Useful Digital Tools for Students  
- Safe Internet Practices  
- Professional Online Presence  
- Email Etiquette  

---

## 🚀 Features
- Easy-to-understand content  
- Real-life examples  
- Awareness-based learning  
- Interactive quiz (optional)  

---

## ⚠️ Importance
Digital literacy is essential in today’s world as it helps individuals:
- Stay safe online  
- Communicate effectively  
- Access information easily  
- Build a positive digital identity
